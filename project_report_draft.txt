2. OBJECTIVE OF THE PROJECT
---------------------------------------------------------
The primary objective of the "CareSync" Hospital Management System is to streamline the day-to-day operations of a hospital setting by digitizing the appointment scheduling process and actively managing critical hospital resources in real-time. The system aims to:
- Establish a secure, role-based architecture bridging Patients, Doctors, and Hospital Administrators over a unified platform.
- Eliminate scheduling conflicts using automated 1-hour time slots with a strictly enforced capacity constraint (e.g., maximum 3 patients per slot) and incorporating standard working hours.
- Bridge the gap between consultation and resource allocation by allowing Doctors to directly assign hospital beds, units of blood from the blood bank, and mandatory medical equipment scans to a patient's digital prescription. 
- Empower Administrators with a bird's-eye view of all hospital operations via a live analytics dashboard reflecting actual availability and expenditure of vital resources, ensuring no equipment double-booking or resource exhaustion.
- Provide a responsive, modern user experience ensuring all participants can securely access, monitor, and update healthcare records transparently.

4. OUTPUT (SYSTEM MODULES & SCREENS)
---------------------------------------------------------
The application features several key output screens representing the flow of the system:
- Output Screen 1 (Landing Page): Introductory premium UI showcasing the "CareSync" brand, outlining system highlights (strict scheduling, role-based access, live tracking), and providing navigation to authentication.
- Output Screen 2 (Authentication Screen): A unified Login and Registration tabbed portal validating credentials to route users into their respective personalized dashboards.
- Output Screen 3 (Patient Dashboard): Includes a guided Appointment Request form (Department -> Doctor -> Date -> Available Time Slot) and a tabular log of their booking history with the ability to withdraw pending appointments or view generated prescriptions.
- Output Screen 4 (Doctor Dashboard): Features an actionable schedule allowing doctors to approve, decline, or complete consultations. The consultation output involves a modal to record diagnoses, prescribe medications, and check-list hospital resources (Beds, Blood Bank, Scans) needed by the patient.
- Output Screen 5 (Admin Dashboard): A comprehensive control panel visualizing real-time metrics of available Hospital Beds, Blood Bank inventory (categorized by blood groups), and Critical Equipment operations via dynamic progress bars. It also provides a global view of all appointments hospital-wide to administer force-cancellations when necessary.
- Output Screen 6 (Printable Prescription & Allocation): A digital receipt view indicating the patient's verified doctor notes, assigned resources, and buttons allowing patients to secure specific slot timings for prescribed medical scans (like MRI or X-Rays).

---------------------------------------------------------
5. CONCLUSION
---------------------------------------------------------
In conclusion, the CareSync Hospital Management System successfully achieves a modernized, structured approach to healthcare administration. By intertwining appointment traffic control with dynamic resource tracking, the application resolves the ubiquitous issue of over-booked schedules and untracked hospital inventories. Transitioning from a manual or disconnected setup to this cohesive Node.js and RESTful architecture ensures data integrity, significantly cuts down patient waiting time, and maximizes the operational efficiency of the hospital’s doctors and administrative staff. The robust design establishes a scalable foundation capable of handling more advanced future enhancements such as billing integration and AI-based triage.
